Who are we?
We are a dynamic organization that offers indigenous services at different stages of a construction life cycle.

With our desktop and mobility applications, we make it easy for the project execution team to collect real time information from the project site. Our cloud platform will then process, analyse and run calculations on this raw data of activity completed, picture evidence and material used to reproduce an actionable construction productivity index. We help organisations monitor the health of its projects in terms of time (SPI) and cost (SPI). as a project management platform. Our real time monitoring takes the help of advanced AI and ML algorithm.
