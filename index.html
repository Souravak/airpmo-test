Our Users

Contractors

From project award, to handing over, every organization faces challenges. These challenges are as diverse as efficient usage of resources, availability of quality materials, or even readiness of documentation. Failure to realize and tackle these challenges early on often results in schedule and budget overruns. With an early flag raising system, real time budget tracking and on the go productivity measurements, you can control your project efficiently, monitor productivity and efficiency accurately, and intervene with pin point precision to tackle challenges as they arise.

Owners

Having visibility and clarity on project metrics is not only important for Contractors, but also for Clients. If your organization is the owner of a project, its sponsor, or even a facility manager, airpmo Con-Tech platform provides you with real-time progress measurements, quality assurance metrics, and more. Ensure your project is on track, within budget, and meets your quality requirements with innovative measurement and reporting tools geared towards a successful achievment of your objectives.

Engineers

Are you a freelance Project Manager? Does your organization provide consultancy services? Do you prepare delay analysis and arbitration reporting? With airpmo Con-Tech platform, your job can be made easier by relying on automated and innovative ways of capturing the required information, displaying it in intutive and informative dashboards, and eliminating the need for tedious study and analysis of periodical reprts to identify root causes of delays, and their effects.
